Your Stair and Railing Experts

The Stair Shoppe was founded in 1988 with a goal of becoming the most competitive and consistent manufacturer of premium woods stairs for the ever-changing Alberta Home Building market. We are a full service stair shop, designing, manufacturing, and installing all types of straight and circular stairs; from construction grade, carpet ready, to hardwood. As part of our commitment to innovation, we are constantly expanding our product lines and refining our production methods to provide options to fit your budget and timeframe.

Our Services

We believe that a great staircase can make a lasting impression in a home. Whether you’re building a new home or renovating, our custom staircases provide excellent quality tailored to fit your budget and deadline. We offer a variety of staircase types, spindles and railings, and a custom stair design service to ensure that our stairs fit in and enhance the identity of your home.
